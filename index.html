How to Play

Basic Care

  • Water: Click the water button when your plant's water level drops below 30%
  • Light: Toggle light to ensure your plant gets proper day/night cycles
  • Fertilize: Use fertilizer once every 5 days for a growth boost

Tasks

Complete daily tasks to earn rewards and help your plant grow faster!

Growth Stages

Your plant will grow through several stages as you take care of it:

  1. Seedling
  2. Small Plant
  3. Medium Plant
  4. Mature Plant
  5. Flowering/Fruiting (depending on plant type)
